An Ode to Cobblers

Cobblers are what make the world go ‘round in the world of stitched footwear. They can skillfully install after-market features like toe-taps or rubber sole protectors. Cobblers are the ones that replace the shitty stock leather soles with glorious JR soles or put real Dainite on your boots.
